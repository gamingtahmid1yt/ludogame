How to Play Ludo

Objective

Be the first player to move all four of your tokens from your starting area to your home area.

Gameplay

Players take turns rolling the dice. On a turn, a player rolls the dice and moves one of their tokens the number of spaces shown on the dice.

Special Moves

A roll of 6 gives another turn. If a token lands on a space occupied by an opponent's token, the opponent's token is sent back to its starting area.
